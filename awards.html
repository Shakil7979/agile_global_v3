Our Achievements

Legacy of Excellence

At Agile Global Solutions, our dedication to innovation, growth, and exceptional service has earned us consistent recognition. Honored among Sacramento’s top IT consulting and technology companies and featured in prestigious national growth rankings, our accolades reflect a legacy of excellence and customer satisfaction.

In addition to these achievements, we are proud to be a Certified Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) by the Western Regional Minority Supplier Development Council (WRMSDC) and CPUC, emphasizing our commitment to diversity and inclusion. As a CMAS-approved contractor, we also demonstrate our reliability and compliance in delivering top-tier services to public sector clients.
